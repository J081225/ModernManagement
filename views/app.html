📧 Property Email Connection

Connect a dedicated property email so resident messages flow into your Modern Management inbox, and replies go out from your real address.

Which email will you use?
⚠ Don't connect your personal email. Use a dedicated address (e.g. mypropertyapartments@gmail.com) so personal mail stays out of your property inbox.

Step 1: Create a new Gmail

  1. Open accounts.google.com/signup
  2. Use a property-focused name like sunsetapartmentsmgr@gmail.com
  3. Set a strong password and enable 2-Step Verification

Step 2: Generate an app password

  1. Go to myaccount.google.com/apppasswords
  2. Create a new app password named "Modern Management"
  3. Copy the 16-character password Google shows you

Step 3: Connect it here

You'll need an app password, not your regular login password. Most providers require 2FA to be enabled first.
