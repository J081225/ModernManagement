SMS Messaging Program — Consent & Terms

SMS Messaging Consent

This page describes how residents and contacts give consent to receive SMS text messages through the Modern Management property management platform, and the terms of that messaging program.

Program Description

Modern Management is a property management software platform used by property managers and landlords to communicate with their residents, vendors, and business contacts. Through this platform, property managers may send SMS text messages to residents and other contacts for the following purposes:

  • Account notifications: Rent payment reminders, lease renewal notifications, and account status updates
  • Customer care: Responses to maintenance requests, inquiries, and service coordination
  • Property operations: Emergency notifications, scheduled maintenance announcements, and building-wide alerts

Consent is Mandatory

SMS consent is a mandatory requirement for receiving any text messages through Modern Management. No messages are ever sent to any phone number without prior, express written consent from the recipient. Consent must be obtained before any SMS communication can occur — this is enforced at both the platform level and the property-manager level.

How Consent is Obtained

Residents and contacts are required to provide explicit written consent to receive SMS messages before any messages can be sent to their phone number. Consent is collected through one or more of the following mandatory processes:

  • Lease agreement (required for all residents): As a mandatory part of the lease signing process, residents provide their phone number and sign a written consent clause agreeing to receive SMS communications from property management regarding their tenancy. This clause is embedded in the lease document and must be signed before the lease is executed. Example consent language: "By signing this lease, I consent to receive SMS text messages from property management regarding my tenancy, including rent reminders, maintenance updates, lease notifications, and emergency alerts. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to cancel at any time."
  • Resident onboarding form (required for all new residents): During mandatory onboarding, new residents complete a form that includes a clearly-labeled, required checkbox: "I agree to receive SMS text messages from property management regarding my tenancy, including maintenance, rent, and property notifications. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to unsubscribe or HELP for help." The form cannot be submitted without checking this box.
  • Vendor service agreements (required for vendors): Vendors providing services to the property are required to consent to receive SMS messages for job coordination and scheduling as part of their service agreement. No vendor receives SMS without signing this agreement.

Consent is not optional. No phone number is ever entered into Modern Management without the contact's prior, express written consent to be reached via SMS at that number. Property managers using this platform agree to our Terms of Service, which strictly require them to obtain and document express written consent before adding any contact's phone number to the system. Failure to obtain consent before sending messages is a violation of our Terms of Service and may result in account suspension.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on the recipient's relationship with the property and circumstances. Typical recipients receive 1–10 messages per month. During periods such as lease renewal, emergencies, or active maintenance issues, message volume may be higher.

Types of Messages You May Receive

  • Rent payment reminders and confirmations
  • Lease renewal notices (typically 60–90 days in advance)
  • Maintenance coordination and status updates
  • Emergency notifications (fire alarms, water shutoffs, etc.)
  • General property announcements
  • Responses to resident inquiries

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan. Modern Management does not charge you for messages, but your carrier may. Contact your mobile carrier for details.

How to Opt Out (STOP)

You can stop receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You will receive one final confirmation message, after which no further messages will be sent to your number. You may also contact your property manager directly to be removed from SMS communications.

How to Get Help (HELP)

For help or questions about SMS messages you've received, reply HELP to any message, or contact:

  • Email: support@modernmanagementapp.com
  • Website: modernmanagementapp.com

Privacy

Phone numbers collected by property managers using Modern Management are used solely for the property-management communication purposes described above. Numbers are never sold, shared with third parties for marketing, or used for promotional messaging outside of the direct property-management relationship. For full privacy details, see our Privacy Policy.

Supported Carriers

SMS messages are supported on all major U.S. carriers, including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and most regional carriers. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
