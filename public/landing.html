Questions

Frequently asked

Do I need to set up Twilio or SendGrid myself? +
For the Pro plan, you'll connect your own Twilio account (for SMS and voicemail) and a SendGrid account (for inbound email). It takes about 10 minutes to configure and we walk you through it step by step. The free plan doesn't require either.
What AI model powers Modern Management? +
All AI features run on Claude by Anthropic — one of the most capable and safest AI models available. It powers your inbox replies, command bar, property reports, and task suggestions.
Can I import my existing residents and data? +
Right now contacts and data are entered manually or come in through inbound messages. CSV import is on the roadmap and coming soon to all Pro and Team accounts.
Is my data private and secure? +
Yes. Every account is fully isolated — no one can see your residents, messages, or financials. Data is stored on a managed PostgreSQL database with SSL encryption. We don't sell or share your data with third parties.
How is Modern Management different from Buildium or AppFolio? +
Buildium and AppFolio are excellent accounting and compliance platforms built for larger property management companies. Modern Management is built differently — it's an AI-first workspace focused on the work you actually spend your days on: tenant communication, maintenance triage, late notices, and resident messaging. Many of our users run Modern Management alongside their existing accounting platform, using it as the AI layer that handles the busywork. If you're a smaller landlord or self-managing owner, Modern Management can stand on its own.
