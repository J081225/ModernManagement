THE COMMAND BAR

One place to run everything.

Most property management software forces you to learn a map of menus. You want to mark a payment? Click three times. Add a contact? Navigate, click, fill out a form, save. Send a late notice? Find the tenant, open their file, draft a message, send.

We threw that out. The AI Command Bar sits at the top of your home screen. It listens for what you want, understands exactly what you mean, and takes action. You don't learn the software — the software learns how you work.

Because it's built on Claude — Anthropic's most capable AI model — it understands nuance, context, and intent. If you say "send a reminder to Alex about the renewal," it knows which Alex, what the renewal date is, and what "reminder" should sound like for a property manager talking to a resident.

UNDER THE HOOD

Full context, every time.

Every time you send an instruction, Modern Management passes the AI a live snapshot of your entire property: every resident, every contact, every task, every rent record, every open maintenance ticket, every upcoming event, every unread message. The AI isn't guessing — it's reading your actual operation before it decides what to do.

That means when you say "mark Unit 4B paid," it knows who lives in 4B, how much they owe, what month's rent you're marking, and whether that resident has a payment history worth noting. It doesn't ask you clarifying questions unless one is genuinely needed.

Under the surface, the AI uses a tool-calling architecture: every capability in the app (create contact, mark rent paid, send message, log expense, etc.) is exposed as a function the AI can invoke. When it decides an action is warranted, it executes it through the same secured API your dashboard uses. Nothing magical, nothing risky — just clean, audited actions.

VOICE CONTROL

Type it. Or just say it.

Property management doesn't happen at a desk. It happens while you're walking to a unit, driving between properties, or standing in a hallway troubleshooting a leak. That's why the command bar has a microphone.

Tap the mic icon and speak naturally. Your words appear in real time, and the AI takes action the moment you stop talking. The voice system uses your browser's native speech recognition (so it works on your phone, tablet, or laptop without any install), and it understands conversational phrasing — not just rigid commands.

Say things like "Log that I spent $340 on the plumber today" or "Remind me to check on Unit 204's AC on Friday" — the AI handles the translation into structured data. No forms, no fields, no friction.
