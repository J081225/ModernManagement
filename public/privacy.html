Modern Management Inc., a Delaware corporation (in formation) ("we," "us," or "Modern Management") operates the Modern Management platform (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information in connection with the Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Username (chosen by you)
  • Password (stored only as a bcrypt hash; the plaintext password is never stored or transmitted after initial submission)
  • Email address (optional during signup; used for account recovery, notifications, and support)
  • Account metadata: creation date, last login timestamp, subscription tier, session identifiers

1.2 Tenant and Contact Data You Upload

As a property manager using the Service, you upload information about residents, tenants, vendors, and other contacts you communicate with. This may include:

  • Names, phone numbers, email addresses, and postal addresses
  • Unit numbers, lease terms, rent amounts, move-in and move-out dates
  • Payment records, outstanding balances, and transaction history
  • Notes, tags, and other metadata you attach to contacts
  • Maintenance history, ticket details, and property-specific information

You are the controller of this data. You are responsible for obtaining any consent or authorization required to upload this information to the Service, including consent to be contacted via email and SMS. Modern Management acts as a processor of this data on your behalf.

1.3 Communication Content

The Service processes the content of communications sent through it, including:

  • Email and SMS messages you send to residents and contacts (inbound and outbound)
  • Draft messages generated by the AI at your request
  • Broadcast content (bulk messages sent to multiple recipients)
  • Internal notes, tasks, and comments
  • Knowledge base entries — property policies, procedures, and documents you upload for the AI to reference
  • Command-bar queries — the natural-language instructions you type or speak to the AI

1.4 Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you use the Service, including:

  • IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Pages visited, features used, actions taken, timestamps
  • Log data generated by our application and infrastructure providers
  • Error reports and diagnostic data

1.5 Payment Information

If you subscribe to a paid plan, our payment processor (Stripe) collects and processes your payment information. We do not store your full card number; we receive only a tokenized reference, the last four digits, the card brand, and the billing status.

2. How We Use Information

2.1 Marketing Communications and Opt-Out

You may receive product-related emails from us, including feature announcements, security alerts, billing notices, and occasional updates about the Service. Transactional emails (account verification, password resets, billing receipts, security notifications) are required for the operation of the Service and cannot be unsubscribed without closing your account. Marketing emails (product announcements, newsletters, promotional content) can be unsubscribed by clicking the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any marketing message, or by emailing privacy@modernmanagementapp.com.

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the Service — authenticate you, deliver features, send messages on your behalf, generate reports, and process payments;
  • Operate AI features — pass relevant workspace context (contacts, rent records, tasks, knowledge base entries, your command-bar query) to our AI subprocessor so it can generate drafts, suggestions, and actions on your behalf (see Section 4);
  • Communicate with you — send transactional emails (account verification, password resets, billing receipts), security alerts, and product updates;
  • Improve the Service — analyze aggregated usage patterns to identify bugs, improve performance, and prioritize features. We do not train AI models on your data (see Section 4);
  • Prevent abuse and enforce our Terms — detect and investigate fraud, spam, TCPA/CAN-SPAM violations, and other misuse;
  • Comply with legal obligations — respond to lawful requests from authorities, preserve records as required, and enforce our agreements.

4. AI Processing Disclosure

When you use Modern Management's AI features, user-submitted content — including resident and tenant communications, property policies uploaded to the knowledge base, and the natural-language queries you type into the command bar — is transmitted to Anthropic's Claude API for processing.

Per Anthropic's Commercial Terms of Service and API Terms, Anthropic does not train its models on data submitted through the API. Your data is used solely to generate a response to your immediate request and is not used to improve, fine-tune, or train any Anthropic model.

In more detail:

  • What is sent to Anthropic: each time the AI is invoked, we transmit a snapshot of the workspace context relevant to the request (e.g., contacts, rent records, tasks, calendar events, open tickets, knowledge base entries) along with your prompt.
  • What is not sent: data from other customers' workspaces, account credentials, payment card numbers, raw session cookies, or database credentials.
  • Transit security: all traffic to and from Anthropic's API is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Anthropic's retention: under their Commercial Terms, Anthropic retains API inputs and outputs for a limited period for abuse monitoring and operational purposes, after which they are deleted. Refer to Anthropic's legal documents for their current policy.
  • No cross-customer leakage: each API request is scoped to a single workspace. The AI never sees another customer's data.
  • Turn it off: if you do not wish to use AI features, you may disable them in your workspace settings. Non-AI features of the Service will continue to work.

4.1 Tenant and Contact Consent

If you upload information about residents, tenants, or other third parties for the AI to process — including communications, lease details, or personally identifiable information — you represent and warrant that you have obtained any consent required under applicable law to do so. Because the AI processes this data to fulfill your requests, your uploaded information passes through Anthropic's API. You are responsible for ensuring your own privacy disclosures to residents and tenants accurately describe this processing, including any AI-driven communication drafting or analysis.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active, and for a reasonable period thereafter, to:

  • Provide the Service to you;
  • Comply with legal, tax, and accounting obligations;
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements;
  • Maintain operational backups.

When you delete your account or specific records, we will delete or anonymize the corresponding data from our active systems within a reasonable period, typically within 30 days. Residual copies may remain in encrypted backups for up to 30 days before being overwritten, and may be retained longer if required by law.

5.1 Data Export

At any time while your account is active, you may request an export of your data in a structured, machine-readable format (typically JSON or CSV). Contact privacy@modernmanagementapp.com to initiate a data export request. We will provide the export within 30 days of a verified request. Upon account closure, you have a 30-day window to request a final export before your data is deleted from our active systems.

6. User Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights with respect to your personal information:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you;
  • Correction — request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information;
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements;
  • Portability — request an export of your data in a machine-readable format;
  • Objection / Restriction — object to or restrict certain processing activities;
  • Withdrawal of consent — where we rely on your consent, withdraw it at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@modernmanagementapp.com. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

Note: if you are a resident or tenant whose information was uploaded to the Service by a property manager, your primary privacy contact is that property manager, who is the controller of your data. We will cooperate with any verified request that comes through the controlling property manager.

6.1 California Residents and Other US State Privacy Laws

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), including:

  • The right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell.
  • The right to delete personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions.
  • The right to correct inaccurate personal information.
  • The right to opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information. Note: Modern Management does not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • The right to limit the use of sensitive personal information.
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights.

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@modernmanagementapp.com. We will verify your identity before processing any request and will respond within 45 days, with a possible 45-day extension for complex requests.

Residents of Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, Texas, and other US states with comprehensive privacy laws have similar rights under their respective state laws. Contact privacy@modernmanagementapp.com to exercise those rights. We will process the request in accordance with applicable state law.

6.2 Tenants and Residents Whose Information Was Uploaded by a Property Manager

If you are a tenant, resident, or other third party whose information was uploaded to the Service by a property manager, the property manager is the controller of your data. You should first direct privacy requests (access, correction, deletion) to the property manager. If the property manager does not respond or you cannot identify them, contact us at privacy@modernmanagementapp.com and we will make reasonable efforts to route your request to the appropriate controller or respond directly where we are required to do so by law.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 16, please contact us at privacy@modernmanagementapp.com and we will take steps to delete it.

8. International Users

The Service is operated from the United States. If you access the Service from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to, stored in, and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country.

International users: At this time, the Service is primarily offered to customers located in the United States, and we have not yet implemented specific compliance mechanisms for the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the UK GDPR, or other non-US privacy regulations. If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction with specific data protection requirements, please contact us at privacy@modernmanagementapp.com before using the Service so we can address your rights under applicable law. We intend to add formal GDPR/UK GDPR compliance measures as our international customer base grows.

10. Cookies and Tracking

The Service uses cookies and similar technologies to operate. Specifically:

  • Essential cookies: We use session cookies to authenticate you and maintain your logged-in state. These cookies are required for the Service to function and cannot be disabled.
  • Security cookies: We may use short-lived cookies for CSRF protection and security monitoring.
  • Analytics: We currently do not use third-party analytics cookies. If we add analytics in the future, we will update this policy and, where required, obtain your consent.
  • Do Not Track: We do not currently respond to browser Do Not Track signals, though we also do not engage in cross-site tracking.

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may prevent you from using the Service.
